# Privacy Policy for Safety QR Code : Scan & Generate QR **Last updated:** May 2026 Nando Faklin ("we," "our," or "us") operates the **Safety QR Code : Scan & Generate QR** mobile application (Package Name: `com.nflx.safetyqr`). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data and permissions within the application. By installing and using this application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. ### 1. Local-First Architecture & Data Privacy Our application is designed with a strict local-first philosophy. We do not own, operate, or maintain any external servers, and we do not collect, harvest, or store any of your personal data or scanning activities on remote databases. * **Camera Permission:** Required solely to process QR codes in real-time. The camera stream is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, transmitted, or shared. * **Storage & Gallery Permission:** Required exclusively to save generated QR code images to your device's local gallery or to share them via third-party systems. * **Local History Tracking:** Your scan and generation history, including text content and timestamps, is stored strictly on your local device storage using `shared_preferences`. You have absolute control over this data and can delete it permanently at any time by clearing the application cache/data. ### 2. Built-in Security Analysis (Pre-Check Link) The application includes a local link verification feature. When a QR code containing a URL is scanned, the application evaluates the string format locally to determine its security status (e.g., verifying HTTPS protocols or flagging direct executable file extensions like .apk or .exe). This analysis is done entirely on-device and does not transmit the URL to external telemetry networks. ### 3. These external services may collect device-specific information to serve functional assets or personalized advertisements: * **Google Play Services** * **Google AdMob:** We utilize AdMob to display banner advertisements at the bottom of the screen and integrated native ads on the result page. AdMob may collect and process data such as your device's Advertising ID, IP address, and interaction logs to serve relevant ads. You can manage or opt-out of personalized tracking through your Android device settings under *Settings > Privacy > Ads*. ### 4.
